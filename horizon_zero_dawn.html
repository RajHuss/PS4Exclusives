Horizon Zero Dawn

In a world without technology, it is technology that rules mankind.

This is the beautiful dichotomy that Guerrilla Games have managed to conceive with their Sony Exclusive Open-World Action RPG Horizon Zero Dawn. The game’s setting would lead you to believe that there have been no technological advancements, as the characters, including the protagonist, wear caveman attire, hunt for food and live in huts. However, they are always on high alert as they share their lands with vicious and dangerous robots, that have been created in the image of animals such as Giraffes, Sabertooth Tigers and Alligators to name a few. The world economy is based around the parts that salvaged from the robots when they’re hunted, and the parts can be used to craft technologically advanced weapons.

The game follows Aloy, a young woman who embarks on a journey discover the mysteries of her world. Her journey will lead her to find why there are robotic beasts, why her civilization have seemingly regressed to its current state, as well as how she is involved in how her society has taken shape.

This game is for the people that love exploring open worlds with plenty to discover as well as the RPG fanatics.
